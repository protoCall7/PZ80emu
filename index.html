PZ80emu

The PZ80 is a homebrew Z80 based single-board computer that I have been designing.

Unable to find a machine emulator that fit my needs, I began writing PZ80emu.

The emulator is currently a work in progress, and still requires quite a lot of effort on implementing the instruction set, the various debug functionality, and the interface. It does, however, work fine as is for simple programs as long as they utilize instructions that have been implemented.
